INSURANCE POLICY DOCUMENT

Policy Number: POL-2024-001
Effective Date: January 1, 2024
Policyholder: John Doe

COVERAGE DETAILS

1. LIABILITY COVERAGE
   - Bodily Injury: $100,000 per person, $300,000 per accident
   - Property Damage: $50,000 per accident
   - Medical Payments: $5,000 per person

2. COMPREHENSIVE COVERAGE
   - Covers damage from theft, vandalism, fire, and natural disasters
   - Deductible: $500
   - Maximum payout: Actual cash value of vehicle

3. COLLISION COVERAGE
   - Covers damage from accidents with other vehicles or objects
   - Deductible: $250
   - Maximum payout: Actual cash value of vehicle

4. UNINSURED MOTORIST COVERAGE
   - Bodily Injury: $100,000 per person, $300,000 per accident
   - Property Damage: $25,000 per accident

EXCLUSIONS

The following are not covered under this policy:
- Intentional damage to the vehicle
- Racing or speed testing
- Commercial use of the vehicle
- Driving under the influence of drugs or alcohol

CLAIMS PROCESS

1. Report the incident within 24 hours
2. Provide police report if applicable
3. Submit photos of damage
4. Get repair estimates from approved shops
5. Claims will be processed within 30 days

PREMIUM PAYMENT

- Monthly premium: $150
- Due date: 1st of each month
- Late payment grace period: 10 days
- Policy cancellation for non-payment: After 30 days
